My project is a music application, which stores songs and a lot of additional data of their lyrics, albums, picture covers, genres and artists.
It contains all description about artists like their biography, members, pictures of these members and from which city and country they are.
I want to develop this project further for final exam and add users. They would have options for creating playlists, add songs and albums, follow their favorite artists.
They could also interact with each other and become friends inside of this application.
I also want to add a song recommendation system for users depending on their music taste.
